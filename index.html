Information

  • Manually add competitors by entering their name and team (optional) into the corresponding entries, then press 'Add Competitor.'
      • If you have regulars, saving a roster, then loading it and wiping scores might be faster.
  • Once all competitors are added, you may press 'Begin Competition' to generate the first round of pairings.
  • Select a winner for each match up during the round. Once the round is complete, press 'Generate Next Round' and the scores will be logged as the next round is generated.
  • The 'Record' can be saved and then loaded at a later date from your device at any time.
      • Note, this will not save the results of current pairings that have not been finalized.
  • Once all competitors have lost twice (except the victor) the match will end.
  • This page does not need to be refreshed once loaded. The 'Record' is saved in the browser cache and won't be lost if the page is refreshed; however, the list of active pairings will be lost (working on fixing this).
  • 'Admin Mode' wipes the list of match ups then let's the user edit the 'Record' table, competitor names and teams. User must exit admin mode before the competition can resume.
